---
title: Portal Hyperspeed
tags:
  abilities:
    - dash
---

If you dash in a portal and then cancel your dash as you enter the portal, you will get a lot of speed when exiting it on the other side. This can be done by jumping either with a double jump if you dash midair or with a regular jump if you dash from the ground but also by taking damage as you enter the portal.
